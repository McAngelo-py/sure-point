Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and procedures.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our main number at +1 (555) 123-4567, using our online appointment form, or emailing us at appointments@surepointdiagnostics.com. Our staff will help you find the earliest available time that works for your schedule.

Do I need a doctor's referral for diagnostic tests?

Most diagnostic tests require a doctor's referral or prescription. However, some health checkup packages can be scheduled without a referral. Please contact our office to verify requirements for specific tests.

How should I prepare for my diagnostic test?

Preparation varies depending on the test. When your appointment is scheduled, you will receive specific instructions. Some tests require fasting, while others may need you to avoid certain medications. Always follow the preparation instructions carefully for accurate results.

How long will it take to get my test results?

Result times vary by test type. Routine blood tests are typically available within 24-48 hours, while more complex imaging studies may take 2-3 business days. Critical results are communicated immediately to your referring physician.

Does Sure Point accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We recommend verifying your coverage with your insurance provider before your appointment. Our billing department can assist with insurance questions and provide cost estimates for services.
